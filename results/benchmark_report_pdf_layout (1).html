Campaign Strategy: Lumina

Executive Summary: Launch strategy for Lumina, an AI-powered smart desk lamp designed to optimize the circadian rhythm and productivity of remote professionals.

1. Market Audit & The Problem

The premium lighting market is currently dominated by standard adjustable LEDs. However, 85% of professionals experience screen-induced eye strain and afternoon fatigue.

2. Strategic Positioning

  • Strategic Pillar: "Light That Works With You."
  • Brand Essence: Lumina is positioned at the intersection of biohacking, productivity, and minimalist design.
  • Market Stance: A premium, scientifically-backed health tool, not just hardware.

3. Target Persona: "Alex"

  • Profile: 25–40 years old. Remote worker, software engineer, or graduate student.
  • Pain Points: Eye strain, afternoon fatigue, and difficulty winding down.
  • Needs: Data-driven ROI on productivity tools. Set-it-and-forget-it automation.

4. Gaps & Opportunities

Very few desk lamps market themselves directly on the health benefits of circadian alignment. We have a unique opportunity to own the "Circadian Health" narrative using data visualization.
