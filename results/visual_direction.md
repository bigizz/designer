# Visual Direction

> Creative guidelines translated from the strategic benchmark report for ICE.

---

## 1. Moodboards

### Theme: "Urban Moroccan Vibrance"
**Concept:** Capturing the authentic, energetic pulse of Moroccan youth culture. This moodboard focuses on real moments, bright sunlight, dynamic angles, and a strong sense of community and joy.
**Visual Elements:**
- High-contrast, sun-drenched photography.
- Street-style aesthetics combined with refreshing beverage cues (condensation, splashing liquid).
- Authentic local environments (rooftops, coastal spots, colorful medina streets updated with modern flair).

### Theme: "Flavor Explosion"
**Concept:** Highlighting the diverse and intense flavors of the ICE product range.
**Visual Elements:**
- Macro shots of fruit and effervescence.
- Bold, solid color backgrounds matching the flavor profile (Orange, Lime, Apple).
- Playful, pop-art inspired compositions.

---

## 2. Color Palette & Typography

### 2.1 Color Palette (Hex Codes)
The palette is deeply inspired by the vibrant flavors and the energetic brand personality.

- **Primary Colors:**
  - **ICE Blue:** `#0055FF` (The core brand anchor, representing refreshment and the classic soda vibe).
  - **Vibrant Orange:** `#FF6B00` (Energy, youth, and the classic orange flavor).

- **Secondary / Accent Colors (Flavor Driven):**
  - **Apple Green:** `#00A651` (Freshness, representing the popular Pomme flavor).
  - **Crisp Lime:** `#A8E000` (Zesty, dynamic).
  - **Passion Red:** `#E6004C` (Bold, intense).

- **Neutrals:**
  - **Off-White:** `#F8F9FA` (For clean backgrounds).
  - **Deep Charcoal:** `#1A1A1A` (For strong, readable typography).

### 2.2 Typography System
To achieve a "Modern, Highly Accessible, Culturally Vibrant" position, we use clean, punchy, and modern sans-serif fonts.

- **Primary Heading Font:** `Outfit`
  - *Usage:* Campaign slogans, large call-outs, primary messaging.
  - *Style:* Bold, Black. It provides a geometric, modern, and friendly feel.

- **Secondary / Body Font:** `Inter`
  - *Usage:* Paragraphs, secondary information, digital UI elements.
  - *Style:* Regular, Medium. Ensures maximum legibility.

---

## 3. Style Guidelines

### 3.1 Imagery Style
- **Authenticity First:** No overly staged or "stock-looking" models. Use relatable, expressive faces of Moroccan youth.
- **Lighting:** Natural, bright, sun-kissed lighting that enhances the vibrant colors of the wardrobe and the environment.
- **Product Integration:** The ICE bottle or can should feel like a natural part of the scene, not forced. Condensation and ice elements should be used to emphasize "refreshment".

### 3.2 Layout & Composition
- **Bold Geometry:** Use solid color blocks or dynamic framing to make the subject matter pop.
- **Typography Integration:** Allow typography to interact with the imagery (e.g., text slightly behind a subject or following a dynamic curve).
- **Whitespace:** Use ample negative space around the product to let it breathe and draw focus, avoiding cluttered compositions.

### 3.3 Tone & Voice in Design
- **Energetic:** Use diagonal lines or dynamic crops to create a sense of movement.
- **Joyful:** Colors should always feel bright and saturated; avoid muted or moody filters.
- **Proudly Local:** Subtle nods to Moroccan culture in styling or backgrounds, without resorting to clichés.
